    Executive Summary

    Corporate International Financial Services Limited (CIFSL) prides itself on having an outstanding team and an advisory board comprised of highly skilled professionals from a wide array of industries. These experts bring rich experience from sectors such as banking, real estate, green energy, and various other sectors. Established in 1995, CIFSL is a Category-I NBFC that specializes in asset-backed lending. The company is strategically expanding its loan portfolio into vibrant sectors like green energy, fintech, education and hospitality Pan India.
